Student Cards

Drag in your gradebook export. Pick a card type — Missing Work for a focused to-do list, or Full Progress for a complete grade snapshot. Everything is read in your browser; nothing leaves your computer.

Step 1 — Load your gradebook

Need to try it out? Use sandbox/fictional-gradebook.csv from this folder.

Step 2 — Confirm columns

Every other column is treated as an assignment. The score format is auto-detected: "Missing", "M", "MI" (any case) count as missing. "Excused", "Exempt", "EX" are skipped. Numbers count as present.

Step 3 — Personalize the message

Use {name} to insert the student's first name.

This is the main note to students — turn-in policy, encouragement, due dates, whatever you want them to know.
