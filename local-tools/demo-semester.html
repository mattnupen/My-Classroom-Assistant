Demo Semester

One semester with Otter — a fictional class, start to finish.

Everything here is fictional — the teacher, students, chats, and numbers. It exists to show what a running project looks like. The same 24 invented students power the "Load demo class" buttons in the other tools.

The class

Mr. Nupen's 8th-grade English

Periods 1 & 3, 24 students. Sweet but chaotic. Problem: missing work snowballs by mid-quarter.

The AI

Otter (they/them)

Named by student vote, beating "Toast" by 4 ballots. Calm coach during the week, big celebrations on Fridays. Mission: every student under two missing assignments.

The schedule

Wednesday + Friday

Wednesday: quick mid-week check-in. Friday: AI Export summary pasted into chat, next week planned.

The semester in one chart

Students per tier, from each Friday's AI Export. Otter only ever saw these counts — never a name.

Generated — semester report (excerpt)

Did Otter move the class toward the mission?

Mostly yes. Students with 3+ missing: 5 → 3 (peak 9 in Week 5). 19 of 24 students finished under two missing.

What worked

Missing-work cards (Week 5), comeback badges (Week 7), strength-first family messages (Week 12).

What didn't

Motivational slides on their own (Weeks 4–5). Friday celebration slides felt stale by December — change them up.

Next semester

Keep the cards, vary the routines, start family messages in Week 6 instead of Week 12.
